Selected Works

Studio RJL is a multidisciplinary design studio crafting bespoke brandscapes that become places.

Rebekah Jane has worked across architecture, landscapes, interiors, objects & visual identities.

Her work is guided by a deep love for natural materials and shaped by a refined sensory experience in tone, texture and gesture.

She designs environments as places with atmosphere, resonance and a sense of elevated whimsy.

For those seeking a brand that feels like a place — immersive, refined, and deeply resonant.
